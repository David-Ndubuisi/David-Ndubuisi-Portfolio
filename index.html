My name is David

(The most Generic name out there lol).

I’m a 19 year old, self-taught frontend developer and creative who enjoys turning ideas into clean, interactive websites and digital experiences. I like things that look good and feel good to use—because what’s the point if it’s ugly or confusing?

Based in Abuja, Nigeria, I spend my time building responsive interfaces, experimenting with animations, and obsessing over small UI details that most people won’t notice (but I will).

My work focuses on frontend development, modern web design, and building interfaces that actually make sense. I also mess around with backend stuff, UI design tools, and whatever new tech catches my attention—because curiosity is half the job.

If you’re working on something cool (or slightly chaotic) and need a developer who cares way too much about pixel alignment, feel free to reach out.
